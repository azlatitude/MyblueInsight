MyBlueInsight Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Overview

MyBlueInsight is a personal mood tracking app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

All mood entries, notes, exercise logs, and settings are stored locally on your device using an on-device database (SQLite). No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Data Storage

  • All data is stored exclusively on your device.
  • We do not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not collect crash reports automatically.
  • The app does not require an internet connection to function.

Data Export

The app provides an optional data export feature that allows you to save your mood data as a JSON file. This file is generated and shared entirely on your device. We never receive or access this data.

Third-Party Services

The app displays painting images from the Art Institute of Chicago's public IIIF API. When viewing painting recommendations, your device may make network requests to load these images. No personal data is sent in these requests.

The app may open external links (Google Images, Wikipedia) when you tap painting search buttons. These are standard web links opened in your device's browser.

Children's Privacy

MyBlueInsight does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
