## Description (4000 chars max)

MyBlueInsight is a simple, beautiful mood tracker that turns your daily emotions into colorful art.

HOW IT WORKS

Pick a color that matches your mood each day. Over time, your calendar fills with a personal color map of how you've been feeling. No complicated forms, no lengthy questionnaires — just tap a color and go.

FEATURES

- Color-Based Mood Tracking: Choose from 12 mood colors including Happy, Calm, Energetic, Creative, Accomplished, Relaxed, and more.

- Beautiful Art Palettes: 6 artist-inspired color themes — Classic, Macaron, Morandi, Monet, Gauguin, and Van Gogh.

- Mood Art Generation: Transform your mood data into unique artwork in 4 styles:
  - Landscape: layered hills and sky
  - Mondrian: color block compositions
  - Fractal Flower: petal bloom patterns
  - Nebula: cosmic clouds and stars

- Painting Recommendations: Discover famous paintings that match your mood profile, powered by cosine similarity matching across 40+ masterworks from artists like Monet, Van Gogh, Picasso, and Hopper. Paintings from the Art Institute of Chicago include viewable thumbnails.

- Weekly, Monthly & Yearly Review: See your mood patterns at a glance with color-coded calendars and distribution charts.

- Exercise Tracking: Optionally log daily exercise (Run, Walk, Yoga, Gym, Swim, Bike, Stairs).

- Data Backup: Export and import your mood data as JSON for safekeeping across reinstalls.

- Dark Mode: Full dark mode support that follows your system settings.

PRIVACY FIRST

All your data stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, no ads. Your mood diary is yours alone.
